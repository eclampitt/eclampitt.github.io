About BlueHound

Chicago-Based Web Design + Developer

The founding of this business began in early 2021. It all started when I began taking online programming courses as a self-lead continuing education initiative with the end goal of increasing my technical knowledge. With a background in engineering it was easy for me to pickup HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

As I learned more about web design and development, I began to see all the different business opportunities available to me if I continued on with the path I was on. It has always been a dream of mine to own a business, something that I built from the ground up. I take great pride in running this business. By partnering with us, know that I will take the time and do the work to provide you a website that you are proud to show your clients.

We tailor our services to meet our customers needs. Every website that we make is designed with our customer in mind.

Emily Clampitt - Founder
