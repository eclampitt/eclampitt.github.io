Dinner Club

About

Dinner Club is a food blog created by two sisters, Tess and Emily. This all began when Tess mentioned how hard it was to cook recipes for only one person and how we should start cooking meals together. Dinner Club started as a way for us to not only spend quality time together but to also share a meal with someone we loved and learn new recipes together. We both alternate hosting Dinner Club and every week we are both asked by friends and family what we are cooking for one and other. This website was designed for us to document what we've cooked so far and share the recipes that we've tested with others. Recipes on this website are both recipes that we've created from scratch as well as recipes that we've tried from other sites. Anyways, thank you for stopping by our blog, we hope that you enjoy our cooking adventures and looking over our recipes!
