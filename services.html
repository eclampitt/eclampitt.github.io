Website Options

Custom Vs. Template Website

There are two main options for launching your own website: building a custom website or using a templated one.

Custom

A custom website is a truely unique product that encompasses the heart and soul of your business. By having a website that is specific to your business and branding you can ensure exceptional user experience for your customers. Websites designed by us are created to ensure ease of use, optimization of brand, and allow you piece of mind knowing that your website is being maintained by professionals.

Conveys your brand's unique value

Improves search engine results

Provides a better User Experience (UX)

Allows for easy scalability

Creates a more professional appearance

Template

Templated websites provide business owners a quick and inexpensive way to get their businesses online. With a templated website you have the ability to adjust your own content and images, but you will be restricted in terms of the design adjustments that can make to your website such as color, navigation, and other elements that are critical for optimizing user experience.
