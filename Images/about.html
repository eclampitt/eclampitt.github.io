WebDesign

Chicago-Based Web Design + Developer

Hello! My name is Emily, and I am the founder of WebDesign. The founding of WebDesign began in early 2021. I started taking online programming courses as a self-lead continuing education initiative with the end goal of increasing my technical knowledge. With a background in engineering it was easy for me to pickup HTML, CSS, and Javascript. As I learned more about web design and development, I began to see all the different business opportunities available to me if I continued on with the path I was on. From there I slowly started to hand-build websites and began to see the very real possibiliy of having a web design and development business.

It has always been a dream of mine to own a business, something that I built from the ground up. WebDesign is that business and it is something that I take great pride in running. By partnering with WebDesign, know that I will take the time and do the work to provide you a website that you are proud to show your clients.
